Services

We offer a wide range of services including primary care, preventive screenings, on-site diagnostics, chronic disease management, and telehealth consultations.

Primary Care

Routine exams, vaccinations, and long-term care coordination.

Primary care serves as the patient's entry point into the health care system and as the continuing focal point for all needed health care services. It involves the provision of integrated, accessible health care by clinicians accountable for addressing a large majority of personal health care needs, developing sustained partnerships with patients, and practicing in the context of family and community.

Imaging & Labs

Quick results with modern equipment and secure reporting.

Diagnostic imaging and lab services use various techniques to view the inside of the body, helping to identify causes of illness or injury and confirm diagnoses. This includes state-of-the-art equipment for CT scans, MRI scans, X-rays, ultrasound, and biopsies, with radiologists specializing in specific types of imaging for high-quality results.

Specialty Referrals

Coordinated referrals to trusted specialists when needed.

Specialty referrals connect patients with specialists for complex care, special tests, or when insurance requires it. They allow primary care providers to share care with experts, providing timely specialist recommendations without undue burden on clinicians or patients.
